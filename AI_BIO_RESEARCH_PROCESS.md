## Search Methodology

### Phase 1: Initial Discovery Searches
For each company, conducted broad searches to identify:
- Company location and office presence
- Official website information
- UK Companies House registration data
- LinkedIn and business profile information

**Search queries used:**
- `"Prima Medicine PrimaMente London office address"`
- `"Charm Therapeutics London office address"`
- `"Valence Discovery London office address"`
- `"Recursion Pharmaceuticals London office address UK"`
- `"Entelo Bio entelobio London office address"`
- `"Hologen AI bio London office address"`

### Phase 2: Postcode Verification
For companies with identified London addresses, conducted targeted searches to verify exact postcodes:
- `"Charm Therapeutics" "7 Pancras Square" postcode`
- `"Valence Discovery" "3 Pancras Square" postcode N1C`
- `"Recursion" "3 Pancras Square" postcode London`
- `Prima Mente PrimaMente London office address street postcode 2026`
- `"Entelo Bio" London office street address postcode`
- `"Hologen" "87 Campden Street" W8 7EN verified`

### Phase 3: Companies House Verification
Cross-referenced all findings with UK Companies House official registry:
- `"Entelo Bio" Companies House UK registered office`
- `site:find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk "Entelo Bio"`

### Phase 4: Social Media and Focus Area Research
Identified Twitter handles and core business focus:
- `Prima Mente Twitter @PrimaMente protein design drug discovery`
- `Charm Therapeutics Twitter handle protein design`
- `Valence Discovery Twitter handle AI drug discovery`
- `Recursion Pharmaceuticals Twitter handle @RecursionPharma`
- `Hologen Twitter handle @Hologen_AI Eric Schmidt`

## Research Limitations

1. **Dynamic Information:** Company addresses can change. Data accurate as of 2026-03-06.
2. **Multiple Offices:** Some companies have multiple locations (e.g., Prima Mente: London, SF, Dubai). Only London offices captured.
3. **Working vs Registered Addresses:** Companies House shows registered office, which may differ from primary working location in some cases.
4. **Twitter Handles:** Social media presence can be informal; not all companies actively use Twitter.
